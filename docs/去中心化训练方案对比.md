## 一、指标体系定义

### 1.0 评估协议分层

| 用途 | Episodes | 说明 |
|------|:---:|------|
| 在线训练监控 | 5 | 低成本趋势观察 |
| Validation 选 ckpt | 10-20 | 避免 overfitting eval seeds |
| **最终 Test 报告** | **30-50** | 确保尾部指标统计显著性, CI 以 episode 为 resample 单位 |

### 1.1 评估指标（`_evaluate()`, steady_window=20）

```
eval_steady_P_D = mean over 5 episodes of:
    mean over last 20 frames of mean(P_D_q across 8 targets)

eval_worst_P_D = mean over 5 episodes of:
    MIN_q (per-target mean P_D in last 20 frames of that episode)
    → Episode-wise: 先每episode取最差目标，再跨episode平均

eval_weak3_P_D = mean over 5 episodes of:
    mean(smallest 3 per-target means in last 20 frames of that episode)

eval_target_std = mean over 5 episodes of:
    std_q (per-target means in last 20 frames of that episode)

eval_full_P_D = mean over 5 episodes of:
    mean(all frames) of mean(P_D_q)
```

### 1.2 训练指标（每 rollout 后）

| 指标 | 计算方式 |
|------|------|
| avg_P_D | mean of all macro-step mean(P_D_q over 8 targets) in rollout |
| comm_agent_var | mean over steps of var(comm_msgs over K agents) — 跨UAV多样性 |
| comm_batch_var | comm_batch.var(dim=0).mean() in PPO update — 熔断用 |
| KL | mean of (ratio-1)-log(ratio) over minibatch |
| entropy | 0.5·log(2πeσ²) mean over minibatch |

> **定义**: `peak_to_final_drop = max(eval_curve) - eval_curve[-1]` (≥0 by definition). 不与 warmup 变化混淆。

### 1.3 联邦聚合指标

| 指标 | 计算方式 |
|------|------|
| Fed Mean | mean(各客户端 eval_steady_P_D @ R8) |
| Fed Worst | min(各客户端 eval_steady_P_D @ R8) |
| 跨区域 Std | std(各客户端 eval_steady_P_D @ R8) |
| Peak-to-final drop | mean(各客户端 best_steady - final_steady) |
| Warmup retention | mean(各客户端 final / warmup) |


## 附录 B：CVaR 目标尾部风险约束 (TRC)

### B.1 动机

30-episode test bank 统一重评表明：DAgger (0.725) 和 Fed (0.716) 平均 P_D 较高，但 **episode 失效率均 >90%**（τ=0.3）。平均性能掩盖了目标级失效。

### B.2 约束形式

不修改原始奖励函数（保持 mean-P_D）。在 reward 层施加独立的尾部风险惩罚：

```
每步计算:
  d_q = max(0, τ - P_D_q)                    # per-target deficit, τ=0.3
  CVaR₀.₇₅ = mean(top-2 deficits of 8)       # ≈ worst 25% tail

  r_k ← r_k - λ_cvar × CVaR₀.₇₅            # 惩罚施加到所有 agent

Lagrangian 双变量更新:
  λ_cvar ← clip(λ_cvar + η_λ × (CVaR - ε), 0, λ_max)
  η_λ = 0.01, ε = 0.05, λ_max = 2.0, λ₀ = 0.1
```

### B.3 与 Bottom-3 奖励的关键区别

| | Bottom-3 奖励 (P2b, 失败) | CVaR 约束 (R1) |
|------|------|------|
| 作用层 | 修改 reward 公式 | reward 外层惩罚 |
| 权重 | 固定 λ=0.1 | 自适应 λ（Lagrangian） |
| 梯度 | 持续施压 | 仅在 CVaR > ε 时增大 |
| 启动 | 从零训练 | 从 DAgger checkpoint |
| 对强目标影响 | 可能牺牲 | 仅在尾部超标时干预 |

### B.4 当前状态

R1 (DAgger + CVaR-PPO, 100ep)：PPO 从 DAgger 初始化时破坏策略，cλ₀=0.1 增长过慢，无法阻止早期退化。需更保守 PPO（LR=1e-5, clip=0.05, KL early-stop ON, cλ₀=0.5）。
